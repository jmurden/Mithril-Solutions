About Mithril Solutions

Mithril Solutions, LLC is a Service-Disabled Veteran‑Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) founded by a U.S. Army combat veteran with a passion for cybersecurity and protecting the American homeland. Our team combines deep cybersecurity expertise with AI‑driven data analytics to fortify America’s critical infrastructure and deliver tangible, lasting results with unwavering commitment. We serve U.S. federal, state, and local government agencies, utilities, and private sector operators. Mithril Solutions brings a blend of innovation and proven security methodologies to craft tailored cybersecurity solutions that combat the evolving threats of today’s cyber landscape.
